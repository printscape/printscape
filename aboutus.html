Who we are?

Our law firm was founded 1991. It is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission and is located at Awka, Anambra State Nigeria. We provide specialized legal services in the varied areas of Accident and Personal Injuries, Estate Planning and Succession, Land Dispute Litigations, Alternative Dispute Resolution, Real Estate Transactions, Chieftaincy Matters, Constitutional Law And Human Rights, Corporate Practice, Taxation, Criminal Defence and Prosecution, Appellate Court Practice, Insurance Claims, Employment and Labour and many more. We are court-room practitioners. We employ cut-edge legal and business technologies and methodologies in providing legal and professional assistance to our clients.

Our Mission?

To provide all-round top-quality legal services, observe high ethical and professional standards and uncompromising dedication to success in satisfying our clients and exceeding their expectations.

Our vission?

To be good and; professionally provide best legal services.
